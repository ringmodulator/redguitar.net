Welcome to my new website! I have a lot planned for 2026, including a duo album with [Sandy Ewen](https://www.sandyewen.com/) on [Sedimental Records](https://sedimental.com/) and a solo album for [SuperCollider](https://supercollider.github.io/) and guitar.

I'm currently based in western Massachusetts and played my first area solo show on March 5, 2026 at [Looky Here](https://lookyheregreenfield.com/), a fantastic non-profit arts center in Greenfield. The show also included a great trio improv set by [Liz Tonne](https://liztonne.bandcamp.com/), [Vic Rawlings](https://www.vicrawlings.com/), and [Bhob Rainey](https://www.bhobrainey.com/).

These days I'm working on my guitar playing, going deeper into digital synthesis and SuperCollider programming, and slowly teaching myself to play the piano. I'm planning to use this website to share information on upcoming shows and releases, and as a personal music blog.
